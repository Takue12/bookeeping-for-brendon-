My Journey

From Zimbabwe to financial expertise - my passion for helping others achieve financial freedom

Roots in Zimbabwe

Born in September 2004 in Zimbabwe, I grew up understanding the value of money and the importance of careful financial management in challenging economic environments.

Passion for Finance

From an early age, I was fascinated by how money works. I taught myself budgeting techniques and helped family members organize their finances.

Education & Growth

As a student, I pursued financial studies while developing practical bookkeeping skills. I learned that organized finances create freedom and opportunity.
